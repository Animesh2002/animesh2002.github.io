2 Things You Didn't Know About Albert Einstein:


  • He never failed in math. This is a popular "fact" promoted on the internet, maybe in an attempt to relate to genius. However it is simple not true. Overall, Einstein wan an average student, but math was one where he excelled, unsurprisingly.

  • He could have been the President of Israel. Where Israel's first president, Chaim Weizmann, died, Einstein was offered the position, but he declined.
